Kubernetes Forum Sydney

2019-12-16

I recently had the privilege to speak about Xero’s Kubernetes platform at Kubernetes Forum Sydney 2019. I focused on how we adopted the mindset of a platform as a product, in order to empower our development teams.

I think it went really well, and received a lot of great feedback afterwards! A recording is now available online for those who missed it.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k3CLW8siogE
