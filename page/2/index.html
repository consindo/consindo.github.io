Bio

As a product engineer, I love to bring ideas to life. I take projects through the discovery process to ensure we're building the right product. A beautiful & thoughtful design comes next, before the engineering & deployment process that enables the vision to become reality. It’s a wide range of disciplines, but it enables the very best outcomes.

Currently at Kiwibank, I’m helping build a cloud platform to enable a new cloud-native bank. Previously, I was working on open banking at Xero, where we enabled millions of customers to automatically import their transactional data every single day.

Alongside work, I love to spend time with family & friends in beautiful Tāmaki Makaurau, Aotearoa. I’m passionate about the climate, transport, and making our cities better places to live.
